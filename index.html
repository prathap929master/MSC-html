About MES Marampally

MES College Marampally is a government-aided college affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. Established in 1995, the college has been reaccredited by NAAC with an A+ Grade (CGPA 3.39), in its IV cycle.

The college is set in lush green surroundings near the river Periyar, close to Cochin International Airport. It offers a significant number of vocational and technical programs aligned with job market demands.
